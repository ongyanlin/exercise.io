Introduction to Exercising at Home

You can start your fitness journey right at home! I started exercising again after my 1.5 year hiatus during the Circuit Breaker period. I started off with no equipment bodyweight exercises. As I grew stronger, I started buying equipment to attempt exercises with weights. During Circuit Breaker period, I bought an exercise mat and resistance bands to supplement my workouts!

There are many different types of exercises you can do at home.

Some of these workouts are:

  • Bodyweight workouts
  • HIIT workouts
  • Dance workouts
  • Shadow boxing workouts
  • Exercise with weights
  • ...and many more!

In this website, I will be focussing on (1) Bodyweight exercises, (2) HIIT and Pilates, and (3) Exercise with weights.
