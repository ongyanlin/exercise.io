Videos of me

Here are some videos of me exercising in the gym!

Running

I usually exercise in the gym for an hour. I start off each gym session with a 30 minute run. Most times, I run a total distance of 3km on the treadmill. I will either (1) jog at a consistent pace for 3km or (2) sprint 100m, walk/jog 200m, then sprint again.

Bent-over Two-arm Dumbbell Row

After running, I'll start on the weight workouts. I usually do a full body workout, and I'll use different dumbbell weights for the different parts of my body. When doing the Two-arm Dumbbell Row, I'll use 7kg dumbbells.

Squats

The dumbbell squat is one of the exercises that I do during my workout session at the gym. In this video, my form is wrong because I couldn't lift the dumbbell properly. But I am getting stronger! And I can lift the dumbbell properly now. Correct form is important during gymming, as improper form can cause injuries.
