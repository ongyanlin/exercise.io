Why Exercise?

Reasons why I exercise

I exercise to relieve stress! Exercising makes me happy and it helps me boost my self-confidence. Exercising also helps me to keep fit and healthy, as it improves my appetite and allows me to tone and grow my muscles.

Benefits of Exercising

There are many benefits to exercising. Some of these benefits include:

  • Helping you to control your weight
  • Keeping healthy, reducing your risk of heart diseases
  • Help your body manage blood sugar and insulin levels
  • Improve your mental health and mood
  • Help keep your thinking, learning, and judgment skills sharp as you age
  • Improved functional mobility
  • Increased energy levels
